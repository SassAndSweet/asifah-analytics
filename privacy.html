Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2025

Quick Summary: Asifah Analytics is a free, open-source intelligence (OSINT) dashboard that aggregates publicly available information. We do not collect personal information, we do not require user accounts, and we do not sell data. We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic and improve the user experience.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Asifah Analytics ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website asifahanalytics.com (the "Site"). By accessing or using our Site, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Asifah Analytics provides geopolitical threat forecasting and regional conflict probability analysis based entirely on publicly available open-source intelligence. Our service is educational and informational in nature and does not represent official government assessments.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not collect personal information. Our Site does not require registration, account creation, or submission of personal data. You can use our dashboard completely anonymously.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, certain information is automatically collected through standard web technologies:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. This includes anonymous data such as:
    • Pages visited and time spent on each page
    • Browser type and version
    • Operating system
    • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
    • Referral source (how you found our Site)
    • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
    Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare: Our Site is hosted and protected by Cloudflare, which may collect:
    • IP addresses for security and performance optimization
    • HTTP request logs
    • Security event data (to prevent attacks)
    Cloudflare acts as a security and performance layer. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Local Storage: We store your language preference (English, Hebrew, Arabic, or Farsi) locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our Site and identify popular features
  • Improve Site performance, design, and user experience
  • Monitor Site traffic patterns and detect technical issues
  • Protect the Site from malicious activity and security threats
  • Remember your language preference for future visits

We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or share any data with advertisers or third parties
  • Use data for marketing or advertising purposes
  • Track you across other websites
  • Create user profiles or behavioral tracking beyond standard analytics

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies primarily for Google Analytics. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand site usage.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics uses cookies to track anonymous usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: Your language selection is stored in localStorage (not transmitted to servers)

How to Control Cookies:

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Consult your browser's help documentation
  • Do Not Track: Our Site does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals, as there is no industry standard for compliance

Note: Disabling cookies may affect some Site functionality, such as language preferences not being saved between visits.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no user data to retain or delete. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default settings (26 months) and is anonymous.

  • Opt out of analytics tracking (see Section 5 above)

7.2 GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect personal data, GDPR obligations are minimal. However:

  • Legal Basis: Our use of Google Analytics is based on legitimate interest in understanding site usage
  • Data Controller: Google Analytics acts as a data processor; Google is the data controller for analytics data
  • Right to Object: You may object to analytics tracking by opting out (see Section 5)

7.3 CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights. However, because we do not sell personal information and do not collect identifiable data, most CCPA obligations do not apply. For transparency:

  • Categories of Information: We collect only anonymous analytics data (see Section 2.2)
  • Sale of Information: We do not sell personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics tracking (see Section 5)
