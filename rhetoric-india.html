About this tracker

The India Rhetoric Tracker monitors absorption-class signals across seven actor clusters: PMO, MEA, Armed Forces, Economic Statecraft, Opposition, Hindutva ecosystem, and Adversary Cross-reads. Calibrated to detect when upstream pressure from Iran, China, Pakistan, and US trackers is actively absorbed into India's domestic rhetorical posture. Scoring measures absorption intensity, not aggression — India's command-node sits where the world's economic stress signals converge.

Data sources

RSS feeds (Times of India, The Hindu, Hindustan Times, Indian Express, Mint, ThePrint, Wire, Scroll, BBC India, Reuters India) · GDELT · NewsAPI · Brave Search · Bluesky (India-specific verified accounts) · Telegram · Native Hindi & Urdu sources · Cross-theater fingerprints from Iran, China, Pakistan, and US trackers · Absorption signatures fire when both upstream pressure AND India's own-signals are present.
