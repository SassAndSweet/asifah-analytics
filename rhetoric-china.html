Methodology

Source-weighted OSINT aggregation with time-decay scoring. Dual-dashboard model: 5 outbound coercion actors (Xi/CMC, PLA Operational, MFA/Global Times, Taiwan Affairs Office, Economic Coercion) + 3 inbound coalition actors (Taiwan Defense, US Commitment, Japan/Regional). Plus regime-level signals (Yuan Internationalization · MFA Dedollarization · BRICS Architect · Sanctions Facilitator · Oil-Yuan Settlement). 12-hour background refresh, cross-theater fingerprint reads from Iran, Russia, and Taiwan trackers.
