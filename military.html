Data Sources

Defense RSS feeds (USNI News, Naval News, Defense News, Janes, Breaking Defense), GDELT global event database (English + 14 language queries including Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Hebrew, Arabic, Farsi, Russian, Ukrainian, Spanish), NewsAPI, Brave Search (tertiary fallback), Reddit (r/CredibleDefense, r/LessCredibleDefence), OSINT Telegram channels, and BlueSky aggregated feeds (POTUS, SecDef, INDOPACOM, OSINT Defender, MOFA Japan, Taiwan MND, ROK MND, and others). Naval positions cross-referenced with public deployment records.

Methodology

Multi-actor military posture aggregation across ~30 state actors and 5 theatres (Global/NORTHCOM, Asia & Pacific, Europe, Middle East, Western Hemisphere) with tiered actor weighting (Tier 1 strike → Tier 4 alliance), asset-class signal classification (carrier strike groups, submarines, strategic bombers, amphibious groups, fighter surges, air defense, ISR, ground forces, logistics, missile tests, naval exercises, base evacuations, drone incursions), regional tension multiplier scoring, and 7-day rolling window. Each actor's signals are scored against country-specific keyword libraries (English + native-language sources where applicable). Naval map shows weighted asset deployments with size = signal weight; Asia & Pacific theatre tracks China, Taiwan, Japan, North Korea, South Korea, India, Pakistan, and Afghanistan.
