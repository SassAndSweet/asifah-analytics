About This Index

Asifah Analytics is a personal-time, open-source OSINT geopolitical intelligence platform. The Western Hemisphere Pressure Index tracks pressure buildup toward instability, conflict, or political rupture across 10 countries using GDELT, NewsAPI, RSS feeds, and regional Spanish/Portuguese sources.

Data Sources

GDELT Project · NewsAPI · Google News RSS · Open-Meteo (weather) · U.S. State Dept Travel Advisories · ME Backend Military Tracker
