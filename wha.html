About This Dashboard

Asifah Analytics is a personal-time, open-source OSINT geopolitical intelligence platform. The Western Hemisphere dashboard tracks conflict probability and instability indicators across 8 countries using GDELT, NewsAPI, and RSS feeds.

Data Sources

GDELT Project · NewsAPI · Google News RSS · Open-Meteo (weather) · U.S. State Dept Travel Advisories · ME Backend Military Tracker

Backend

asifah-wha-backend.onrender.com · Updates every 12 hours · Force refresh via Scan OSINT
