About This Dashboard

Asifah Analytics is a personal-time, open-source OSINT geopolitical intelligence platform. The Western Hemisphere dashboard tracks conflict probability and instability indicators across 8 countries using GDELT, NewsAPI, and RSS feeds.

Data Sources

GDELT Project · NewsAPI · Google News RSS · Open-Meteo (weather) · U.S. State Dept Travel Advisories

Backend

asifah-wha-backend.onrender.com · Updates every 12 hours · Force refresh via Scan OSINT

⚠️ Research & Educational Use Only This dashboard is not affiliated with any government agency. Conflict probability scores are algorithmic estimates derived from open-source data and should not be used as the basis for any operational, security, or policy decisions. Not for official use.
