About This Tracker
  • Cross-cutting commodity intelligence for the 5 actors most exposed to strategic resource pressure: Belarus, Russia, China, Israel, Ukraine.
  • Sparklines pulled from Yahoo Finance via yfinance. News signals from GDELT (6 languages), NewsAPI, RSS, Reddit, and Brave Search fallback.
  • Potash has no public spot price — tracked via production volume + sanctions cycle.
Methodology
  • Tier-1 commodities (oil, gas, wheat, potash) carry the highest signal weight.
  • Country pressure scores apply per-commodity exposure weighting (producer vs consumer vs transit).
  • News scan runs every 12 hours; sparklines refresh hourly.
  • "Top Signal" pill on each card reflects the highest-weighted news entry from the last scan.
