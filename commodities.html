Data Sources

Yahoo Finance (sparklines via yfinance), GDELT (6 languages), NewsAPI, Mining.com Direct RSS, Reuters/Bloomberg/FT (Google News), USGS, USDA, Reddit, Brave Search fallback. 15 strategic commodities tracked across 32 countries in 5 regions.

Methodology

Tier-weighted signal scoring (Tier 1 = 1.0, Tier 2 = 0.7, Tier 3 = 0.5). 7-pattern analytical classifier: supply disruption, demand surge, cost structure, cross-commodity, market regime, geopolitical coupling, consolidation. News scan every 12h; sparklines refresh hourly. Multi-role schema (v1.3.0) supports countries that are both producer and consumer of the same commodity.
