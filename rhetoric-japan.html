      Methodology

      Dual-dashboard architecture: 6 inbound threat actors (China, DPRK, Russia, Senkaku intrusions, Okinawa pressure, Taiwan Strait spillover) + 5 outbound posture actors (PM Cabinet, MOFA, MoD/JSDF, LDP/Diet, US Alliance).
